About Me

Hi, I’m Africa! I am an Occupational Therapist and Software Engineer specializing in assistive technology, full-stack web development, and yoga therapy

My passion is working with technology to increase accessibility for neuro-typical individuals as well as those with cognitive, physical, and emotional needs. My desire is to be an advocate for users of technology and build meaningful relationships rooted in finding creative solutions to technical problems.

I use web development tools to create dynamic web applications using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Express, MongoDB, and nodeJS. I have extensive experience with collaboration and supervision of multi-functional teams, teaching and presenting complex ideas and concepts, sections 504 and 508 of the ADA, and inclusion. I share yoga tips for computer users, as well as talk about my journey merging therapy and technology on my YouTube channel.

I am interested in increasing innovation and usability for virtual tools and platforms using universal designs that can benefit people of all abilities. I look forward to expanding my knowledge and reach in the technology and healthcare industry as a therapist, software engineer, and accessibility specialist.
